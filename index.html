About Us

LIFEGAINS is a social utility and lifestyle company. We harness people's social capital to increase their access to essential daily needs including foods, clothing, shelter, mobility, tourism, education and health. We responding to the downturn in the standard of living as we see human resources as drivers for the integration of a vibrant society. Our strategy is to help participants acquire the needed skill and resources to interconnect and leverage on their social capital.
